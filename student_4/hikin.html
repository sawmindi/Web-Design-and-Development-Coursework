Haputale

Haputale is a small town located in the Badulla District of the Uva Province in Sri Lanka. It is situated at an elevation of 1431 meters above sea level and is known for its cool climate and picturesque landscapes.The town is surrounded by rolling hills covered with tea plantations, and the area is a popular destination for nature lovers and hikers.

Ella Rock

Ella Rock is a popular hiking destination in the town of Ella, Sri Lanka. The 8km moderate to difficult hike takes about 3-4 hours and offers stunning views of the surrounding hills and valleys. At the summit, you'll be rewarded with breathtaking panoramic views, especially during sunrise or sunset. It's a must-visit destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.

Baker Falls

Baker's Falls is a 20-meter-high waterfall located in Sri Lanka's Horton Plains National Park. The hike to the falls is about 3.5 km long and takes about 1-2 hours. Visitors can enjoy stunning views of the surrounding landscapes and wildlife along the way. It's a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts and photographers.

Knuckles

The Knuckles Mountain Range in central Sri Lanka is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a popular destination for hiking, trekking, camping, and other outdoor activities. The landscape is characterized by rugged mountains, rolling hills, and lush forests, and is home to a wide variety of plant and animal species. It's a must-visit destination for nature and adventure enthusiasts.
