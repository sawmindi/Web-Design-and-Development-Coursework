Negombo beach



Negombo, Located six kilometres away from Bandaranayake international airport of Sri Lanka, is a style predominantly fishing hamlet with slender roads and little boutique Hotels and many historic shrines to attend and observe, with its routes going after the advent of the Portuguese. Negombo's beach, which extends north from the city right along the hotel strip, is fading toward a palm-tree background, is fair sufficient and offers an immeasurable sunset

Uppuveli beach-Trincomalee



Uppuveli Beach has located only 3km from Trincomalee town. Uppuveli offers an outstanding balance between the palm tree-laden sandy beach and waterfront bars and an array of accommodation options. Uppuveli looks straight East onto the Indian Ocean, making it another perfect location on the East to view an oceanfront sunrise. Best season February - November

Passikudah beach



Passikudah beach is located in the Eastern seaboard of Sri Lanka near Kalkuda, about 35 kilometres from Batticaloa Town. The sea-green blue waters of the bay attract local and foreign tourists to this vast sandy beach under the hot tropical sun to surf, swim or frolic in the water. Several additional activities are available, like kite surfing, boogie boarding to surfing and sailing and more. It is presented very emerging as a tourist hot spot in the East of Sri Lanka. Coconut palms and palmyra trees adorn the shores of this area; due to the semi-arid state, Sunscreen and a large-brimmed hat are recommended along with lots of liquid to keep the heat under control.

Bentota beach



Bentota beach is environed by bush and palm trees. The sand is bright white on this vast beach, and the sea is bright aqua-blue due to its light-coloured sandy bottom. It's the place for many Star class Hotels gathered together plus numerous other more petite boutique hotels with Sun, surf and sand. This provides the guest with a perfect opportunity to stay in one and experience several water activities. Bentota has always been famous as the water games capital of Sri Lanka, with the hoteliers in the area actively promoting water sports from an adrenaline-filled speed boat and jet ski riding, windsurfing, boogie boarding, water skiing, kite surfing to a leisurely ride on a banana boat for the group & kids.
